Our Mission

The FAYE team is passionate about improving the lives of students and providing them with the materials that they need to succeed.

Our Plan

Not only do we provide free access to our future advice panels and informative seminars, but we plan to hold book drives and sessions where our members interact with children. This process will be executed after the COVID-19 pandemic comes to an end.

Our Vision

We hope to witness students of all backgrounds, ethnicities, and races flourish in schools as well as possess the help that they need to do their best.
