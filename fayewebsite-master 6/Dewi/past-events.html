FAYE Workshops (May 26th to May 29th):

FAYE and AHS clubs have partnered together to hold a week of free virtual workshops from May 26 to May 29. Each workshop focused on a unique set of skills from film to genetics. FAYE Political Volunteering Infographic (July 2nd):

To reach out and encourage the youth of our generation, FAYE created an infographic about all the ways teens can volunteer during the pandemic for the election year. It was meant to help students find a new passion during the summer while giving back to their community. The infographic reached over 200 people. Art was made by Keerti Varada, Research by Saanvi Agarwal

FAYE is determined to help our community during the Covid-19 pandemic. We are proud to present a fundraiser partnered with the Center for Disease Control (CDC). All proceeds went to help our community of doctors and medical workers.
