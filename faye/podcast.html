About the Podcast

Welcome to FAYE ~Foundation for Advocating Youth Education~ a nonprofit organization that aims to provide all students with a safe and efficient educational environment. Hosted by a group of high schoolers, ConverFAYEtional is a podcast that dives into various aspects of a student’s daily life. Our podcast discusses issues that students face right now, from school to home. Our main goal is to document what it is like to be a student in the 2020 school year, as it is a very different experience than what most of us expected. If you are wondering what it is like to be a high-schooler during the pandemic, want something to relate to, or just want more information on the education process, please tune in via Apple podcast and Spotify!
